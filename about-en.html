About FH TRADING LIMITED

Founded in 2004, FH TRADING LIMITED has been specializing in premium women's evening dress manufacturing and wholesale for 20 years. Headquartered in Hong Kong, we own our factory and professional R&D team, providing high-quality evening wear to clients worldwide.

We have accumulated rich experience in international OEM/ODM manufacturing and have established long-term stable partnerships with many renowned brands. Whether it's wholesale, cross-border supply, dropshipping or custom manufacturing, we can provide professional solutions.

The company has a complete quality management system and relevant authoritative certifications, ensuring that every product meets international standards. We adhere to the philosophy of "Quality First, Customer First" and are committed to creating value for our customers.
